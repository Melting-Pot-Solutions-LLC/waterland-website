Water Resource Management

Successful irrigation, water supply, sanitation, flood control, and hydropower depend on how wisely we use our water resources. Our water resource management team focuses on fostering sustainable economic activity, improving or sustaining environmental quality, ensuring public health and safety, and providing for the sustainability of communities and ecosystems.
