NEPA

Under the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA), federal agencies are required to assess the environmental effects of their proposed actions prior to making decisions on permit applications, adopting federal land management actions, and constructing publicly-owned facilities.

Cultural Resources

Cultural resources are also known as heritage assets and include archaeological sites, buildings and structures, landscapes, objects, and historic documents. Federal, state, and local agencies are required to consider and limit negative effects that a project will have on cultural resources.
